# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and professional community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Trolling, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to security@mvar.dev.
Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations.
